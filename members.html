Taeyong (이태용)

Rapper, singer, dancer, and songwriter. He is best known as a member and leader of the boy group NCT.[1] In 2016, he debuted in the group's first sub-unit, NCT U, and later that year went on to become the leader of the second sub-unit, NCT 127

Mark Lee (이민형)

Mark Lee , known professionally as Mark, is a Canadian rapper, singer, songwriter, dancer, and host of Korean descent. He is a member of the South Korean boy group NCT and its fixed sub-units NCT 127 and NCT Dream, and is also a member of the supergroup SuperM.
